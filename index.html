Admissions & Enrollment

We accept applications on a rolling basis. To secure your seat in the upcoming batch, please follow these steps:

  1. Review the individual course syllabi linked above to select your preferred track.
  2. Ensure you meet the specified prerequisites for the desired program.
  3. Navigate to our Student Registration Form.
  4. Complete all required fields and submit your registration.
  5. An admissions advisor will contact you within 2 business days to schedule an interview.
